Detailed Description

template<typename TGridFunc>
class ug::Electromagnetism::EddyCurrentHeat< TGridFunc >

UserData based class that computes the field of the heat sources \(Q = \frac{1}{2} \sigma \mathbf{E} \overline{\mathbf{E}}\), where \(\overline{\mathbf{E}}\) is the complex conjugate to \(\mathbf{E}\). The electric field \(\mathbf{E}\) in the grid function should be represented by the Nedelec-type-1 elements.
