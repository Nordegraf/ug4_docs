Detailed Description

Truncated EquiDistant Lagrange Function

Creates for given order p and iterpolation point i the polynomial

\[ \prod_{j=0}^{i-1} \frac{x - \frac{j}{p}}{\frac{i}{p} - \frac{j}{p}} \]
