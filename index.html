ug4 - Documentation

About

ug4 has been written and is still developed at the Goethe Center for Scientific Computing at the University of Frankfurt, Germany (http://www.g-csc.de).

What is ug4?

ug4 is a simulation framework, targetted at the solution of differential equations on unstructured hybrid grids in 1, 2 and 3 dimensions on systems reaching from laptops to massively parallel systems. ug4 is written in C++, striving for a flexible, yet fast and robust simulation environment ...
